Welcome to IMO2023

The Mathematical Olympiad Foundation (IMO) is pleased to confirm that the 64th

International Mathematical Olympiad will be held in Chiba on July 2-13, 2023.

Hosted by

The mathematical Olympiad Foundation of Japan

Co-hosted by

Japan Science and Technology Agency (JST)

In association with

  • Ministry of Education, Culture. SportsScienceand Technology(MEXT) - Japan
  • Mathematical Society of Japan
  • Japan Society of Mathematical Education
  • Chiba Convention Bureau and International Center (CCB-IC)
